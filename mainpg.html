Trekking Adventures

Trekking is a long, adventurous journey on foot, usually in natural and mountainous regions. It involves walking for multiple days across trails, hills, valleys, and forests, often reaching beautiful and remote places not accessible by vehicles. It is not just about walking—it’s about exploring, experiencing nature, testing your limits, and often meeting local cultures.
